Organic Cashew Milk
Vanilla - 1.54 Liter

Caiü's Vanilla Cashew Milk is crafted with 4 simple ingredients: sustainably sourced, organic cashews, vanilla extract, maple syrup, and water. That's it. Far from simple however, is its nutritional profile and decadent flavor. Boasting 8 grams of protein per serving, less sugar than dairy milk, and oil that's naturally high in Omega 3 fatty acids, our vanilla cashew milk is not only wholesome, it delivers a subtly nutty, rich vanilla flavor that pairs well with everything and lends it true drinkability.

The result of over a year of research and development, Caiü's milk products have been tuned to perfection for your enjoyment and health.
